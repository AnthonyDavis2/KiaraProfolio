Important Details

* BOOK BY EMAIL/ TEXT FOR SUNDAY- WEDNESDAY. APPOINTMENTS FOR THOSE DAYS VARY. DAY OF THE SESSION


DAY OF THE SESSION:

You agree that you will be dressed in your outfit, and arrive on time to the chosen location. A 10 min grace period is given, unless stated otherwise by the photographer. If you are late showing up to your scheduled appointment, you will automatically have to reschedule. Friends/ family are welcome, so long as they do not interfere with the photographer/ photography taking place.


PAYMENT AND PACKAGES:

Payment towards the rest of the package will be required on the day of shooting. You will not receive any pictures, nor will editing start, unless full payment of the entire package is made. Cash, online payment (paypal/ cashapp) is accepted. You may not downsize to a smaller package after you have paid the deposit for that package. However, you may upgrade to a larger package BEFORE photoshoot has begun, IF CLEARED with photographer.

A further payment of all necessary expenses (unless otherwise specified) such as parking, travel time, etc must be paid the day of shoot. The Client shall reimburse the Photographer for any additional costs the Photographer may incur for travel, parking, and other reasonable costs necessary to the performance of these services. Cost of location permits for shooting/ availability and the choosing of a location is the responsibility of the client.


RECEIVING YOUR PROOFS AND RETOUCHED PHOTOS:

For small sessions, please allow time after the shoot to choose the amount of pictures stated in the package you chose. If you can’t, please wait up to 7-14 days from the date of your session to receive your proofs. Allow up to another 7-14 days after you’ve selected your photos for retouching.


PROOFS:

Proof selection is sent digitally and must be chosen within 7 days of receiving your proofs. Failure to do so will result in delayed process of your photos. After 30 days of no selection, your order will be deferred and the photographer will choose your “top” photos from the session. Proofs are not meant to be full resolution and are used as a reference for the photos to choose for retouching. Your selection must be sent through by photo number only. Photos from the proofs are unedited and may not be posted online for any reason, if so, it will result in a breach of contract.


RETOUCHING TIME AND PROCESS:

The retouching process begins once proofs are selected. Clients who choose their proofs first will get their photos retouched first, regardless of when their photos were taken and which package they have. Any approved retouched photos may not be considered for re-editing. All changes are final once approved and any extra retouching desired by the client is subject to separate charges per revision.


COLORING, RETOUCHING AND STYLES:

Basic retouching includes clearing skin, getting rid of flyways and single strands of hair in the face. It does not include things like physically changing body shapes, fixing clothes, removing people from a photo or adding things to it. By choosing Kaptured by Ki you acknowledge the style and colors used and agree to allow the photographer to choose the colors based on personal preference.


OVERALL APPEARANCE AND PERSONAL ITEMS:

The photographer is not responsible for any missing, damaged or altered clothing/material belonging to the client. Nor is the photographer responsible for any styling, makeup issues, hair problems or any other factors in the session other than the directing and photographing of the client. Please be aware that for some photos you may or may not get dirty. It is ultimately your responsibility to make sure your clothes, makeup, hair and overall appearance are the way you want for the photos.


WEATHER AND SESSION CHANGES:

You are able to switch your day because of weather or personal reasons, so long as no other sessions are scheduled that day and it works for the photographer. Locations within 15 minutes of the Photographer’s city are free. Any other location outside 15 minutes of it is subject to a separate charge based on distance.


DIGITAL FILES, CDs AND EXTRA PRINTS:

All files will ordinarily be sent digitally through dropbox or email and will be made available for download.


REFUNDS:

In the event of an emergency, equipment failure or other unforeseen circumstance, the photographer will return any money given up to that point but claims no further liability.


EXCLUSIVITY:

It is understood and agreed that no other photographer will be allowed to photograph or take pictures while the Photographer is working. Guests shall use their cell phones as long as they shall not interfere with the Photographer’s duties.


PHOTO REORDERING AND TIME FRAME:

You may reorder more photos from your session by purchasing your digital files. To keep organized and conserve space, your photos may be deleted or unavailable 90 days after your session has taken place, and you waive any and all claims against Kaptured by Ki of the unavailability of such photos, even if you must replace them at your own cost. Therefore, it is recommended you pick your poses out and finalize your choices with the photographer as soon as possible.


FINALIZATION:

You will be contacted and made aware when your photos are ready, but it is your responsibility to check your email for your proofs or retouched photos.
